Frequently Asked Questions

Is the $99.99 a one-time purchase?

+

Yes! The core Anvil software and marketplace access are yours forever with a single purchase. The Guild Pass subscription is an optional add-on for those who want exclusive monthly content and premium features.

Does the AI run locally on my computer?

+

Absolutely. The default AI models run 100% locally on your machine, ensuring your conversations and data remain private. The optional Guild Pass may offer access to more powerful cloud-based models, but local-first is our core principle.

What are the system requirements?

+

We are optimizing Anvil to run on a wide range of modern PCs. Generally, you'll need a dedicated graphics card (NVIDIA GTX 1060 / AMD RX 580 or better) and at least 8GB of RAM for a smooth experience.

What 3D file formats can I import into the Forge?

+

The Anvil Forge supports all major 3D formats, including .FBX, .OBJ, and .GLTF. Our goal is to make it as easy as possible to bring your existing creations into the Anvil ecosystem.
